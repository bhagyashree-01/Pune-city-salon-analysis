# Pune-city-salon-analysis

Objective of the Report:
The primary objective of this dashboard is to analyze the salon industry across Pune and Pimpri-Chinchwad by providing a comprehensive view of salon distribution, categories, and geographic coverage. It helps identify high-demand areas, understand market concentration, and support data-driven decisions for business expansion, marketing, and resource planning.

Overview of the Dashboard:
The dashboard presents key metrics such as total areas covered, number of salons, beauty parlours, academies, and mix salons. Interactive filters for area, pincode, and contact availability allow users to explore data dynamically and focus on specific locations or business types.

Key Insights Provided:
-Area-wise analysis highlights regions with the highest number of salons, helping identify competitive and underserved locations.
-Pincode-wise distribution shows salon density across different pincodes, supporting location-based planning and market penetration strategies.
-Contact and address-level details enable easy access to salon information for outreach, partnerships, or operational analysis.
-Comparison between Pune city and Pimpri-Chinchwad reveals differences in salon concentration and business mix across regions.

Business Value:
This dashboard enables stakeholders to understand market coverage, identify growth opportunities, and optimize location-based strategies. It is useful for salon owners, investors, marketing teams, and analysts looking to evaluate regional trends and make informed strategic decisions.
